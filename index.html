INFO

Hi & thanks for being here. My name’s Alma, an Indonesian creative currently based in Naarm / Melbourne. I am well skilled in many areas of communication design through my Bachelor of Design degree that I completed at Monash University, combined with my 2+ years experience working in the industry.

I have love for anything & everything visuals, bringing brands to life with the right energy. My design processes are research based and client centered. With collaborating at the forefront, I always approach projects with a fresh mind, adding just the right amount of spice while still making sure we deliver the objectives to take your brand to the next level.

When I’m not designing, you can find me pretending I’m in Masterchef, listening to my (current) favourite music, or enjoying the sun sets
