KANYE VS THE PEOPLE — 2018
This poster series responds to controversial tweets of Kanye West and how people responded to it.
I developed a publication (or a creative journal) documenting the screenprinting
and creative process to go along with this project.












Double-sided two colours screenprint on tracing paper.
