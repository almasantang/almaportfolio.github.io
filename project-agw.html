ALL GOOD WORKS — 2020
All Good Works started as a business in party/ event decoration but now have decided to step into a more professional market through rebranding themselves as an event organiser.
The objective of this rebranding was to capture a broader and more mature audience by showcasing an elegant, flexible, and unique brand. A full brand analysis was done to identify the area which needed to be worked on.
