AGORA — 2020
Agora creates a space for writers and creators to share bits of the pieces they’re from and put forward new, bold ideas. By being tirelessly interested in the present moment, Agora aims to create a publication that patches together a global perspective on what makes the young(ish) person tick, what they’re curious about, and what they hold out hope for.
Agora is a Greek word which means a public open space used for assemblies and markets. The main objective of this task was to create a logo that encapsulates Agora’s energy. We agreed on making it look contemporary and minimal, yet still fun.
