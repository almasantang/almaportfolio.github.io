This is a promotional campaign run by Sand & Sky to promote their head band. It is a part of the Spring/Fall campaign. The idea behind the creative is to capture the “season changes” through animation.
Some of the assets that were made for this campaign include homepage banners extension, EDMs, short-form and long-form ads.
