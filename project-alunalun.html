ALUN ALUN — 2019
Alun - Alun is an annual event held by PPIA (Indonesian Student Association in Australia) to welcome new students, as well as to promote Indonesian culture in Victoria. Through art and culture, this event aims to provide entertainment and increase the interest of Indonesian people in Victoria as well as locals to explore further the richness of art and culture owned by Indonesia.
The theme itself is to bring the essence of the old Indonesian era throughout the years so that the today’s youth of Indonesia can feel the vibe of the old Indonesia, as well as the previous generations, can reminiscent. The creative for this event is based on the theme of Alun - Alun 2019, “Indonesia Tempoe Doloe” which means Indonesia in the old days. It is inspired by classic folklore illustration.
