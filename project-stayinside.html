STAY INSIDE — 2020
Stay Inside is a quick motion result from what is currently happening in the world. I felt very emotional when I made this, which was right after my sister texted me of how scared, stressed out, and anxious she is at the moment. My sister is one of the many heroes who’s treating the COVID-19 patients – A big thank you to all of you out there!
